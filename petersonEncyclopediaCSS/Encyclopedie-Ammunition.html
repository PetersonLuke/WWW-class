Ammunition

The main way to kill tanks

Standared and Premium Ammo

In World of tanks there is Standared Ammo, payed for with credits, and Premium ammo payed for with large amount of credits, or gold.

There is a 5 common types of ammo used in World of tanks

  • Armor Piercing (AP)
  • Armor Piercing Composite Rigid (APCR)
  • High Explosive (HE)
  • High Explosive Anti-Tank (HEAT)
  • High Explosive Squash Head (HESH)

Armor Piercing:

Armor Piercing is the most common round used.
AP rounds are the best round vs. sloped armor, as they have a normilization of 5% and will auto bounce at angles over 75°. As AP rounds travel, they will lose penetration over distance.

Armor Piercing Compiste Rigid

APCR in lower tiers is more often Premium ammo, in the higher tiers it seen often as standared.
APCR is better Vs. flater armor but will fair okay Vs. sloped armor with normilization of 3% and will auto bounce at angles over 70°. As the APCR rounds travel, they will lose a large amount of penetration over a distance.

High Explosive

HE rounds specilize in dealing large amounts of damage, but has low penetration.
He rounds don't care what armor, as they will always explode on contact. They also don't lose any penetration over distance, but travel slow.

High Explosive Anti-tank

HEAT rounds have the highest penetration out of all the rounds, but are almost always premium ammo (with a few exeptions).
HEAT rounds are best to use Vs flat armor and tanks at long ranges, as they don't lose any penetration over time, but have slow travel time. They have no normilization and will auto bounce at angles over 85°.

High Explosive Squash Head

HESH rounds are only available with British tanks and are only really used with the FV183. They act like HE but have better penetration.
