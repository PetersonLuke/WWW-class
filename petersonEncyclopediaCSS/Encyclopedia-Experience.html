Experience

Experience is earned on each Tank and is used to upgrade components and unlock the next tier of tank.
You can see your experience located in the top right corner of the garage.

Ways to Earn Experience

  1. Deal Damage to tanks
  2. Spot tanks
  3. Get assistance damage
  4. Capture the base
  5. win the match
  6. don't die

Free experience

5% of all experience earned is changed into free experience that can be used to help unlock stuff.
