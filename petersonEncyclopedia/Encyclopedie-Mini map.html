THE MOST IMPORTANT TOOL!

How to use the mini map

  • At the start press + to make it larger. (As shown)
  • Outer ring is max render range
  • The white ring is the max view range
  • The inner ring is the view range of your tank
  • Dashed line is the derection your gun is pointing
  • Friends are green
  • Enemy (not show) are red
