Credits

Credits, or silver, are the common in game currency. They are used to buy tanks, Ammunition, along with other odds and ends. You can see how much you have in the top right of your garage.

Ways to earn credits

  1. deal damage to other tanks
  2. Win Matchs
  3. Do Missions
  4. Don't die
  5. Penetrate with all the rounds you fire
